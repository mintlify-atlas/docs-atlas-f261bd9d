## Welcome to Glide Browser

Glide is an extensible and keyboard-focused web browser built on Firefox. It brings Vim-like modal editing and powerful TypeScript configuration to your browsing experience.

## Key Features

### Navigate with Hints

Press `f` to enter hint mode and overlay labels over every clickable element. Type the label to activate links, buttons, and interactive elements without touching your mouse.

### Commandline Interface

Open the commandline with `:` to execute commands, switch tabs, and access powerful features. Think of it as your browser's command palette.

### Built on Firefox

Glide is built on top of Firefox, giving you access to Firefox's rendering engine, security features, and compatibility with Firefox extensions.

### Fully Extensible

Customize Glide's behavior with TypeScript configuration. Create custom keymaps, excmds (commands), autocmds (event handlers), and more.

## Who is Glide for?

Glide is perfect for:

- **Keyboard enthusiasts** who want to browse without a mouse
- **Vim users** looking for familiar modal editing in their browser
- **Power users** who want deep customization options
- **Developers** who appreciate TypeScript configuration
